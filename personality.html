Travel

This was a really long time ago, but this is a picture of me in 2014 when I went to Bangladesh for the first time in my life. I don't remember much but this was a one in a lifetime experience as I had so much fun seeing my family. Some memories that stuck up to me from this experience was when I went fishing, as fishing was popular in Bangladesh. I also went to see how a average day of school was im Bangladesh, and it was really different. I noticed how the curriculum is quite old and how the students are some shenanigans, like I remember one time when some kid decided to waste the whole powder and drop it on the floor, and teachers used to hit people, so when it was time for me to go home, I remember the teacher had a stick to probably hit or scare the student for his misdoings. Well that was quite an interesting experience. I actually went a year ago back in 2023 to Bangladesh, and it was still really fun, but I did enjoy the first time I went as I was baffled at many of the things uncommon in the West.
